Dpowcoin Core 26.2 is now available.

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT! ALWAYS BACK UP YOUR WALLET DATA BEFORE ANY UPDATE.**
> Shut down your wallet completely, then copy your `wallet.dat` file to a safe place before
> updating. If possible, always encrypt your wallet with a strong password.

**Fix Network sync problems**

## Troubleshooting

- **"Unexpected application id" error** — ensure you are using the 26.2 binary, not an older one.
- **Balance shows zero after rescan** — wait for the rescan to fully complete:
  `dpowcoin-cli getwalletinfo` → check the `"scanning"` field.
