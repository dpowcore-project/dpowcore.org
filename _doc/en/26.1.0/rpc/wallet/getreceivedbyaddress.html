--- name: getreceivedbyaddress dpcversion: 26.1.0 dpcgroup: wallet permalink: en/doc/26.1.0/rpc/wallet/getreceivedbyaddress/ --- getreceivedbyaddress "address" ( minconf include_immature_coinbase ) Returns the total amount received by the given address in transactions with at least minconf confirmations. Arguments: 1. address (string, required) The dpowcoin address for transactions. 2. minconf (numeric, optional, default=1) Only include transactions confirmed at least this many times. 3. include_immature_coinbase (boolean, optional, default=false) Include immature coinbase transactions. Result: n (numeric) The total amount in DPC received at this address. Examples: The amount from transactions with at least 1 confirmation > dpowcoin-cli getreceivedbyaddress "dpc1qztyqhx06t6gf40v8kdmd84s6ty9jkex8cfn94p" The amount including unconfirmed transactions, zero confirmations > dpowcoin-cli getreceivedbyaddress "dpc1qztyqhx06t6gf40v8kdmd84s6ty9jkex8cfn94p" 0 The amount with at least 6 confirmations > dpowcoin-cli getreceivedbyaddress "dpc1qztyqhx06t6gf40v8kdmd84s6ty9jkex8cfn94p" 6 The amount with at least 6 confirmations including immature coinbase outputs > dpowcoin-cli getreceivedbyaddress "dpc1qztyqhx06t6gf40v8kdmd84s6ty9jkex8cfn94p" 6 true As a JSON-RPC call > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id": "curltest", "method": "getreceivedbyaddress", "params": ["dpc1qztyqhx06t6gf40v8kdmd84s6ty9jkex8cfn94p", 6]}' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:42002/
