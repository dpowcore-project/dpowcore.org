Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What is a cookie

A cookie is a small text file a website can ask your browser to store, then send back on later requests to the same site. Session cookies are removed when you close your browser; persistent cookies remain until their expiry date. Third-party cookies are set by services other than the site you're visiting — for example, a CDN or a DDoS-protection layer in front of it.

Cookies on this site

The code in this repository does not set any cookies of its own — there is no analytics, tracking, or session-management script here. Any cookie your browser shows for this domain comes from the infrastructure the site is served through, not from this project's code:

  • Hosting (GitHub Pages). GitHub's own infrastructure serves this site and may set cookies as part of that service. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • DDoS / edge protection. If this domain sits behind a reverse proxy or DDoS-protection service, that service may set a cookie to distinguish legitimate visitors from abusive traffic.

Your choices

You can configure your browser to accept, block, or delete cookies. Blocking cookies from the infrastructure above should not affect this site's core content, since none of it depends on cookies set by this project. Blocking cookies required by a DDoS-protection layer, if one is in front of this domain, may in some cases prevent pages from loading.

Consent

By using this site with cookies enabled in your browser, you consent to the cookies described above being set. If you'd rather not, you can disable cookies in your browser, though this may affect pages that depend on infrastructure-level cookies (such as DDoS protection, if present).
